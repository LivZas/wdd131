Resources

The following resources can help with mental health in case you need suggestions or ideas for feeling healthier and happier.

Hotlines

The hotlines are a very important resource for emergencies about mental health. If you need to call one, you can click here.

Professionals

Professionals such as psychologists, psychiatrists, and therapists can help you by listening to your problems and providing important tools for mental health.

Talking to a Beloved One

Talking to family, friends, or even your peers can help you vent and distract you from the challenges that life may present or from very stressful moments.

Attending Church

Praying to the Lord, reading the Bible, and attending church to find a relaxing moment and be at peace can be a perfect way to relieve anxiety and stress. Additionally, it is a good way to address your problems with our Heavenly Father, and I know that He will always be there for us.
