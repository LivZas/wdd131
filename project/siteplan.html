Project Site Plan | Mental Health Shack

Site Name | Mental Health Shack

Why did I choose this name? The way this name is composed can be taken as cozy and as a safe place to learn about mental health and feel relaxed. Like people reading in a shack.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide valuable information about mental health.

Scenarios

Why seeking for help is important?

Some people may believe that mental health is less important than other aspects of life, often succumbing to the stress, anxiety, and depression that arise during difficult times.
Seeking for help in the right time can be a life saver and we can learn a lot by reading useful articles about mental health.

What is the best way to learn about mental health?

There are many articles, videos, and other resources available online. Many people don't know where to find them or may not know how to locate professionals like psychologists or psychiatrists.
